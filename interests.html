    • What is a VT-Shaped Student to Me?

    I view a VT-Shaped Student to be someone who is involved in campus activites and community service all while maintaing good academic standing. I believe that participating in activites outside of the classroom is where students gain the most insight and knowledge. Anyone can sit in a class and attempt to learn a subject, but it takes a truly motivated student to particapte in learning outside of the classroom.

    I also believe in Ut Prosim and try to exemplify it in my daily life. A VT-Shaped student is someone who will always lend a helping hand and voluenteer for causes near to their heart. Virginia Tech students are very philanthropic and I think that sets us apart from most other schools.
